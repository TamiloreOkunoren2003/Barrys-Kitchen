Our Story

Celebrating Authentic
Nigerian Cuisine

At Barry's Kitchen, we celebrate authentic Nigerian cuisine by crafting homemade meals infused with passion. Our commitment is to bring the vibrant flavors of Nigeria to every table — from your everyday comfort meal to your grandest celebration.

Based in Fulshear, Texas, we serve families, corporations, and event planners who believe food is more than sustenance — it is culture, memory, and joy shared.

What's Cooking

Our Menu

Jollof Rice

Smoky, flavour-packed jollof made in an open pot for that authentic party taste.

Basmati Fried Rice

Colorful, fragrant fried rice loaded with fresh vegetables and a rich umami depth.

Singapore Noodles

Stir-fried vermicelli noodles with shrimp, vegetables and a bold, aromatic spice blend.

Ofada Rice

Indigenous unpolished Nigerian rice with a distinct earthy flavour, served traditionally.

Yam Porridge

Rich, spiced yam porridge slow-cooked with palm oil, crayfish and seasoning.

Ewa Agoyin

Mashed black-eyed beans served with a bold, caramelised pepper and onion sauce.

Signature Ata Rilli

Our signature stew — bold, richly spiced peppered sauce with deep layers of flavour.

Ofada Sauce

The classic designer stew — fermented locust beans, assorted meats, and fiery pepper blend.

Efo Riro

Rich Yoruba spinach stew slow-cooked with palm oil, assorted meats, crayfish and peppers.

Efo Elegusi

Hearty melon seed soup cooked with leafy greens, palm oil, assorted meats and crayfish.

Chicken Stew

Tender chicken slow-simmered in a rich tomato and pepper stew base.

Turkey Stew

Succulent turkey pieces cooked down in a well-seasoned tomato stew.

Assorted Stew

A hearty blend of assorted meats cooked down in a rich, deeply seasoned stew base.

Chicken

Oven-roasted and peppered chicken — a crowd-pleasing classic at every event.

Turkey

Whiting Fish

Tilapia Fish

Fried Plantain

Gizdodo
